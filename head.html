HTML - Heading and Paragraphs

 

Headings

In this article, we will know HTML Heading, & its implementation through the examples. A HTML heading tag is used to define the headings of a page. There are six levels of headings defined by HTML. These 6 heading elements are h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, and h6; with h1 being the highest level and h6 being the least.

Syntax:

<h1>Heading 1</h1>
<h2>Heading 2</h2>
<h3>Heading 3</h3>
<h4>Heading 4</h3>
<h5>Heading 5</h5>
<h6>Heading 6</h6>
 
Output:

Heading 1

Heading 2

Heading 3

Heading 4

Heading 5
Heading 6
